What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

I learnt that I compare myself to others and their learning journey, even though I understand the detrimental effect of this kind of thinking, I still find it hard to switch myself critical voice off. I also realised I have bad habits in terms of balance, I will work for an extended period of time and forget to take breaks, because of this I have found myself looking at syntax and complicating it, instead of breaking it down.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self - awareness in learning and programming?

I think that the role of values, empathy and self - awareness are important aspects of anything that a person devotes themselves to. Its extremely important to understand your values, it allows you to operate with integrity.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I am surprised how much I am incorporating my learning into my everyday life. I reference back to in conversations with others, and find myself practicing what I have learnt during unexpected times.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

I find it difficult to switch into my growth mindset, I am still struggling to get my head around some of the JavaScript syntax and I am finding it difficult to keep my optimism and keep in the mindset that I am capable of learning this skill.

Why do you think that we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

Because these core skills are just as valuable as technical skills. The ability to understand yourself translates into the way you interact with others and with your work. It is a valuable skill to have good habits and a healthy mindset, it not only benefits yourself but contributes to unity of your teams.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

I think that it was valuable time spent. This learning will continue to serve me in the future and I will use this learning in many aspects of my life.
